About Me

I am a Medical Oncologist with specialized training in the diagnosis and treatment of all types of cancer. My clinical focus spans solid tumors and hematological malignancies, with a deep commitment to both active treatment and cancer prevention strategies.

My approach to oncology is rooted in three pillars: precision medicine, patient-centered care, and continuous learning. I believe that early detection and prevention are as powerful as treatment — and I work actively to educate patients, families, and communities about cancer risk reduction.

Beyond clinical work, I am passionate about research and translating scientific findings into meaningful improvements in patient outcomes. I regularly engage with the latest oncology literature to ensure my practice reflects the best available evidence.
