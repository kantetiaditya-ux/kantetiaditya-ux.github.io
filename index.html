About Me

I am Dr. Aditya Pavan Kumar Kanteti, a Medical Oncologist currently practising at Omega Bhimavaram Hospitals, Bhimavaram, Andhra Pradesh. My training spans three of India's most esteemed medical institutions — Andhra Medical College, Visakhapatnam (MBBS), PGIMER Chandigarh (MD, Internal Medicine), and Tata Memorial Centre, Mumbai (DM, Medical Oncology).

Prior to my current role, I served as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Medical Oncology at Tata Memorial Centre, Mumbai — one of Asia's foremost cancer hospitals — where I was involved in patient care, teaching, and clinical research at the highest level.

My clinical philosophy is rooted in the belief that cancer is best fought on two fronts: through precision-guided treatment for those already diagnosed, and through proactive prevention, early screening, and community education for those at risk. I am committed to bringing the standards of care I trained under to the people of Andhra Pradesh and beyond.
